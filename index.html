About Me

I currently am in the process of moving so I am a bit of a nomad right now. Previously, I lived in the United Arab Emirates and the Philippines. I also lived in almost every part of the US, including Texas, Michigan, Mississippi, Maryland, Utah, Tennessee, and Oregon.

I am an opera singer turned nuclear engineer turned entrepreneur. I have a bachelor's degree in mathematics and a master's in nuclear engineering focused on quantum/subatomic physics. Now, I run companies teaching computer programming, AI/ML, and quantum computing to people around the world. I am also currently going to school myself to learn cyber security. In 2025, I joined BYU Pathway as a student in Software Development, which I am really enjoying.

I serve as both a religion instructor and as a missionary for Pathway Connect gatherings in the European and Eurasia Areas. I served my first mission in the Russia Yekaterinburg Mission (a long time ago). I have served in a few stake presidencies, many bishoprics, and taught many classes for all ages. I have taught about 50 religion and institute classes. My life was blessed by amazing institute teachers in my younger years. I pay tribute to them by teaching myself now.
